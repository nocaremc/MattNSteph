Welcome to our karate world!

Welcome to our world of karate! Thank you for stopping by our site. Here you can learn more about our lives as we start another season of karate tournaments. We would like to introduce you to our world of traditional Japanese karate, Shito-ryu style and our intensive training 6 days a week as we prepare for local, regional, state and national championships.

As you learn more about karate, the training, and the travel involved, we hope you will sponsor us so that we can continue to travel and compete in tournaments throughout the country and the world!
